Tips & FAQs

How do I search by ingredients?

If you want a meal with a specific ingredient, you can search up that ingredient and the app will populate every meal that has that ingredient.

How can i save my favorite recipes?

You have to create an account and sign-in in order for the app to save your progress.

Where can i find nutrition facts?

Nutrition facts populate at the bottom when you click on a meal.
