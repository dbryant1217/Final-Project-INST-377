Tips & FAQs

How do I search by ingredients?

If you want a meal with a specific ingredient, search that ingredient and the app will show recipes connected to it.

How can I save my favorite recipes?

Click the Save Favorite button on a recipe card. The recipe is sent to the backend and saved.

Where can I find nutrition facts?

The recipe cards show the basic recipe details provided by the recipe API.
